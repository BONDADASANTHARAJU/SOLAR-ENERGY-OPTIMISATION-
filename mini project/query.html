Frequently Asked Questions

1: Why is solar energy more reliable in rural areas?

Solar power provides consistent electricity, even during weather-related power outages.

2:How complex is solar infrastructure setup?

Solar systems are simpler to install compared to other renewable sources, and off-grid solar setups can be DIY-friendly.

3:What economic opportunities does solar energy offer?

Solar projects create jobs and stimulate local economies in rural regions.

4:How does solar energy impact health?

By replacing kerosene lamps, solar power improves indoor air quality and reduces health risks.

5: What environmental benefit does solar energy bring?

Solar is eco-friendly, reducing carbon footprint and conserving water resources.
