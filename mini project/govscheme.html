    • 1. Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha Evam Utthaan Mahabhiyaan (PM KUSUM)

    AIM:

2.Surya Ghar:

AIM:

This is a scheme that aims to provide free electricity to households in India. Under this scheme, households are provided with a subsidy to install solar panels on their roofs.

3.Pradhan Mantri Suryodaya Yojana:

AIM:

aimed at making electricity bills of poor and middle-class citizens ‘zero’ while helping them make substantial gains from t he rooftop panels installed on their terrace.
